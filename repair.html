Repair Services

Do It Yourself (DIY)

Fix your bike with our tools.


• Limited handsoff guidance provided.


• $10 / hour [ members ].


• $20 / hour [ non-members ].

Do It Together (DIT)

Fix your bike alongside our mechanic.


• Hands-on guidance provided.


• $20 / hour [ members ].


• $30 / hour [ non-members ].

Mechanic

Our skilled mechanic will fix your bike.


• 15 & 30 minute service options.


• $14 / $28 [ members ].


• $20 / $40 [ non-members ].

Repair Approach:

We prioritize teaching and fixing over replacing.

Repair Appointment Availability:

Repair appointment times open seven days in advance.

Repair Appointment Cancellations:

Please cancel with at least 1 hours notice.

Repair Limitations:

One bike per appointment & prices do not include parts.

We Do NOT:

• Service hydraulic brakes.

• Install tubeless tires.

• Rebuild or repair suspensions.

• Diagnose or repair ebike components

• Provide complete rebuilds.

Members Discounts:

30 - 50%.

Appointment Preparation:

A clean bike is faster, easier, and cheaper to repair.

Walk-In Repair Services:

Welcome but not guaranteed!
